About Us

We strive to bring you something different in a podcast over the several ‘Complaining about Current Product’ shows you see everywhere. Just story’s from the diehards...sharing opinions with you.

Chris "CuZ" Prescott

Cuz is still technically a newly wed and this is his brain child...basically he thought we had something unique to say to a audience of Wrestling Fans...and as we have progressed so has the sound quality, outside edits, and audio ‘run ins’ due to his efforts. Though he may seem quiet at times, Cuz has his moments that make the podcast unique to say the least, and many of the great things you hear post edit are his creations.

David "The Narrator" Butterfield

Married and a father of two, this clown has the least amount of talent and knowledge of the three, but manages to put together a decent storyboard, keeping Jess and Cuz on track so the audience can follow what the three of us are trying to convey. But really he is an overrated wrestling researcher, trying to have fun with his friends...but he won’t hesitate to kick these guys back on track when necessary.

Jess "The Heel" Burch

Married with two lovley children, Jess is an encyclopedia of wrestling knowledge. Believe it or not, Jess could tell you the History of the WWF World and Intercontinental Championships from memory up to close to the churn of the century...then some of that stuff gets ridiculous...but that’s impressive. It’s a passion of Jess’ to find any dirt sheet, social media story, true accounts, and great matches in order to make your listening experience the best possible...however don’t be surprised when he goes Heel on us over something that truly needs to be called out or debunked...which is probably the funniest things we will capture in recording sessions.
